Skipping Legs

Legs release the most testosterone and growth hormone. Squats and RDLs directly improve your overall physique.

All Chest, No Back

Creates hunched posture and kills V-taper. Always pull as much as you push — back width is everything.

Ego Lifting

Heavy weight with bad form equals injury, not muscle. Start light, master the movement, then add weight progressively.

Ignoring Side Delts

Shoulder width creates the V-taper illusion. Hit lateral raises 2× per week — they respond well to frequency.

Poor Sleep

Muscles grow while you sleep, not in the gym. 7–8 hours is your most powerful free supplement. Don't skip it.

Only Cardio for Fat Loss

Cardio alone burns muscle. Combine lifting + high protein intake + mild cardio for true body recomposition.

Inconsistency

3 months of consistent effort beats 1 month of perfect training. Show up every week, even on low-energy days.

Guessing Protein

Even rough tracking on Healthify Me or MyFitnessPal is 10× better than guessing. Hit 160g protein daily, non-negotiable.
